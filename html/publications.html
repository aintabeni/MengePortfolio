Publications

As a researcher in water resources engineering, I have focused on integrating field data, modeling tools, and data science approaches to improve water resource management, watershed-scale hydrologic modeling, and stormwater systems. My work aims to provide decision-makers with the tools and insights needed to manage water quality and availability in the face of changing environmental and climate conditions.

Below is a selection of my peer-reviewed journal articles. A full and updated list is available on Google Scholar.
